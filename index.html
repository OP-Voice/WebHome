Overland Park Voice

A Platform for Community & Commerce

Our Mission: Completing the Civic Dialogue

To champion a prosperous, future-ready Overland Park by fostering collaboration between our businesses, homeowners, and the entirety of our community workforce. We believe that long-term economic vitality is built on a foundation of community well-being, strategic investment, and opportunity for all residents.

Overland Park benefits from many avenues for resident participation. However, a significant portion of our community—the people who make up our community workforce—currently has a limited channel to formally contribute to this dialogue. Overland Park Voice seeks to be an additive force, bringing the perspectives of this vital group to the table to ensure the city’s decisions are informed by the full spectrum of community experience.

Our Core Principles

Collaborative

We seek to build bridges, not walls, believing the best solutions are found when all stakeholders work together toward shared goals.

Community-Focused

A city's success is measured by the success of all its people. We are committed to ensuring the perspectives of our community workforce are part of the civic dialogue.

Data-Driven

We advocate for policies based on transparent, verifiable data that highlights the true costs and benefits of development decisions for the entire community.

Action-Oriented

We are focused on ensuring a community-centric vision for Overland Park is not just heard, but enacted through positive, constructive engagement.

Our Platform: Aligning with City Goals

In May 2024, the City of Overland Park adopted FrameworkOP, its official comprehensive plan. The platform of Overland Park Voice is fundamentally aligned with this vision. Our role is to act as a constructive partner to ensure the city is successful in transforming its well-articulated policies into tangible reality for all residents.

Pillar 1: Housing for Our Community Workforce

Goal: To ensure Overland Park has a diverse and attainable range of housing options that support our community workforce.

Key Initiatives:

  • Activate the Overland Park Land Bank to preserve attainable housing.
  • Establish a clear and fair "Renters' Bill of Rights" for housing stability.
  • Champion "missing middle" housing to diversify our housing stock.

Pillar 2: Fiscally Responsible Growth

Goal: To ensure the city's development process is transparent, predictable, and yields a clear, measurable return for the community.

Key Initiatives:

  • Advocate for a transparent, public rubric for evaluating projects requesting public incentives.
  • Promote Community Benefit Agreements (CBAs) to give the community a direct voice in its growth.

Pillar 3: Empowering Community Stewards

Goal: To build a robust pipeline of knowledgeable and engaged residents who can participate constructively in civic life.

Key Initiatives:

  • Create "The People's Leadership Program" (PLP) as a civic incubator to train and support residents.
  • Encourage broad-based appointments to city boards and commissions to ensure leadership reflects the community.

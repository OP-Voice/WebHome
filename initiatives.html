Current Initiatives

Our active advocacy campaigns focused on making local government more accessible and responsive.

Current Focus

Government Transparency

Making City Hall Accessible to All Residents

The Challenge

Many residents don't know who represents them, when meetings occur, or how to participate in local government. This creates barriers to civic engagement, especially for working families and renters.

Our Approach

  • Comprehensive local government directory
  • Clear meeting schedules and participation guides
  • Simplified ward finder tools
  • Accessible contact information for all officials

Values Alignment

C
Connecting residents with their representatives
A
Removing barriers to civic participation
D
Providing clear, factual government information
R
Measurable increase in civic engagement

Current Focus

Housing Task Force

Ensuring Housing Remains Affordable for Our Community Workforce

The Challenge

While 38% of Overland Park residents are renters, formal city engagement structures primarily serve homeowners. Rising housing costs threaten to price out the teachers, retail workers, and healthcare staff who power our local economy.

Our Approach

  • Advocate for a Housing Advisory Commission
  • Include renters, landlords, and housing advocates
  • Provide formal input on housing-related policies
  • Present data-driven testimony to city council

Values Alignment

C
Bringing together diverse housing stakeholders
A
Creating formal representation for renters
D
Using salary and rent data to inform policy
R
Concrete policy recommendations
